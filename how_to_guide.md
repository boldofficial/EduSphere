# Registra: Step-by-Step How-To Guide
**Your Practical Manual for Mastering the School Ecosystem**

---

## 1. For Administrators: School Management

### How to Customize Your School’s Identity & Branding
1.  Navigate to **Settings** (located in the **Tools** category).
2.  **Branding & Signatories**:
    *   **Upload School Logo**: This will appear on dashboards and reports.
    *   **Watermark**: Upload your school’s crest for report card backgrounds.
    *   **Signatures**: Upload the Director’s and Head of School’s signatures for automated validation on documents.
3.  **School Profile**: Update your school name, address, and official contact phone/email.
4.  **Session & Display**: Set the **Current Session** (e.g., 2025/2026) and **Current Term**.
5.  Click **Save Changes** at the top right to apply.

### How to Configure a Custom Domain (Enterprise Only)
1.  Ensure your **DNS settings** are configured: 
    *   Create a **CNAME** record pointing your custom domain (e.g. `portal.school.com`) to `app.myregistra.net`.
    *   Wait for DNS propagation (usually 30 mins to 2 hours).
2.  Navigate to **Settings** > **Domain Management**.
3.  Enter your custom domain in the **Custom Domain** field.
4.  Click **Save Changes**.
5.  Users can now access your portal directly via `https://portal.school.com`.

### How to Manage Your Public Website (CMS)
1.  Go to **Website CMS**.
2.  Update the **Hero Title** and **Subtitle** (visible on your school's landing page).
3.  Configure **Core Values**, **Academic Programs**, and **Testimonials**.
4.  Set your **Primary Brand Color** to ensure the public site matches your school colors.
5.  **Nigerian Curriculum Support**: Set up classes with specific streams (Science, Art, Commerce) in **Classes** and assign individual subjects to students in **Students**.
6.  Click **Save Changes** and visit your school's subdomain to see the updates!

### How to Manage Academic & Support Staff
1.  **Academic Staff**: Go to **Teachers** to manage instructors and their profiles.
2.  **Non-Academic Staff**: Go to **Non-Academic** to manage support, admin, and operational staff.
3.  Click **Add New Member** to create a profile. This automatically generates their system login and sends them an invitation.

### How to Run Monthly Payroll
1.  Navigate to **Bursary** > **Payroll**.
2.  Click **Generate Draft** for the current month.
3.  Review the automated calculations (Basic Salary + Allowances - Deductions).
4.  Click **Approve Payroll**.
5.  When funds are disbursed, click **Mark as Paid**. You can also choose to automatically record this in **Bursary** > **Expenses**.

## 2. For Academic Officers: Data & Scheduling

### How to Generate a Conflict-Free Timetable
1.  Ensure **Classes**, **Subjects**, and **Teachers** are correctly setup.
2.  Go to **Timetable**.
3.  Click **Magic Generate (AI)**.
4.  **Registra AI** will instantly build a schedule that avoids teacher and class clashes.
5.  Review the slots and click **Save** to publish to the school.

### How to Process End-of-Term Results
1.  Verify that all scores are entered in **Grading**.
2.  Check the master overview in **Broadsheet**.
3.  Go to **Grading** > **Generate Report Cards**.
4.  Choose the **Session** and **Term**.
5.  Use **Promotion Autopilot** (found in **Settings** > **Session & Display**) to set the pass threshold for automatic student advancement.

---

## 3. For Teachers: Daily Workflows

### How to Record Attendance & Scores
1.  **Attendance**: Open **Attendance**, select your class, and mark students present or absent.
2.  **Grading**: Open **Grading**, select your subject, and enter CA and Exam scores. The system handles all grade letter assignments automatically.
3.  **AI Remarking**: Within the grading view, use **Draft with AI** to create high-quality, personalized teacher remarks based on the student's performance data.

### How to Manage Digital Learning & CBT
1.  **Learning Center**: Upload lesson notes, videos, or reading materials for your students.
2.  **Quizzes (CBT)**: 
    *   Create a new quiz in the **Learning Center**.
    *   Use **AI Quiz Generator** to turn any lesson text into a set of MCQ questions instantly.
    *   Set the timer and publish for students to take online.

## 4. For Parents: Transparency & Support

### How to Pay Fees Online
1.  Log in to the **Registra Parent Portal**.
2.  Navigate to **Bursary**.
3.  Select an outstanding invoice and click **Pay Online**.
4.  Once the payment is successful (via Paystack), a receipt is instantly generated and your balance is updated.

### How to Access Student Reports
1.  Go to **Academic Records** or **Broadsheet** (depending on your access level).
2.  Select the term and click **View Report Card**.
3.  You can view teacher remarks, attendance trends, and performance charts online or download the PDF.

## 5. Support & Troubleshooting
*   **Issues?**: Use the **Support & Tickets** module to send a direct message to the school administration.
*   **Password Reset**: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen.
*   **System Help**: Access the knowledge base at `help.myregistra.net`.
